This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI on the popular Sample Superstore dataset.
The dashboard provides meaningful business insights related to sales performance, profit analysis, regional trends, customer segments, and discount impact.

Dataset Used
Dataset Name: Sample Superstore Dataset
Data Type: Retail Sales Transaction Data
Key Fields:
Order Date
Sales
Profit
Discount
Quantity
Category & Sub-Category
Region & State
Segment
Ship Mode

Dashboard Features & Visualizations :

🔹 KPI Cards
Total Sales
Total Profit
These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business performance.

🔹 Sales Trend Analysis (Line Chart)

Shows yearly growth in sales, helping understand business expansion over time.

🔹 Profit by Sub-Category (Bar Chart)

Identifies most profitable and loss-making products, supporting strategic product decisions.

🔹 Sales Distribution by Region (Pie Chart)

Highlights regional contribution to total revenue, useful for regional planning.

🔹 Sales by Category (Bar Chart)

Compares performance of Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies.

🔹 Segment-wise Sales (Funnel / Bar Chart)

Shows which customer segment (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office) contributes the most revenue.

🔹 Discount Analysis (Bar & Pie Charts)

Analyzes how discounts are distributed across categories and regions, and their possible impact on profit.

🔹 Quantity Analysis by Region

Helps understand product demand in different regions.

🔹 Matrix Table (Detailed Report)

Provides a tabular business summary showing sales/profit across categories for deeper analysis.
